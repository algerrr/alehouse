Thank you for visiting our website. We want to give you the opportunity to stay in touch with Cascade Locks Ale House promotions and specials.
The Ale House changed ownership in November 2012 and was formally named Pacific Crest Pub and Hostile, Salmon Row, Suzie's Suds Locker, Lakeside Inn and a few more.
Good News!!! We are open a lot. You can stop by and eat almost every night. Open Wednesday through Monday. Closed on Tuesdays. Please come check us out!
We appreciate all the local support and visitors that are passing through.
Family friendly establishment with kid's meals and diaper changing accommodations. Also dogs are always welcome outside.
